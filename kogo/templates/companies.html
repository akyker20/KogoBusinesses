How our Service Works

Pricing that makes sense

Pricing is simple and painless. You provide us with an advertisement and you only pay when students actually see your ads.

At Kogo, we charge you by the impact second. An impact second is one second that a student spends watching your advertisement. This way your every penny goes towards gaining a potential customer.

We are unique in that we can guarentee you that your advertisements are only being shown to your target market segment and that you are only paying when they see your ad. You won't find a more efficient and cost-effective service on the market.
