University Students.

How our Service Works

  • You provide us with an advertising clip you want to show students.
  • We offer students free rides around campus and to nearby locations.
  • During these rides, students watch your clips on video monitors in the vehicles.
  • Students learn about your products and services and are influenced to try them out.

Why Students love us.

We offer students free rides between campuses and to nearby off-campus locations.

With Kogo, students no longer have to wait long periods of time for buses in order to travel between campuses and do not have to pay expensive taxi fares to go off-campus. We put students in a good mood so they can positively absorb your advertisment.

Pricing that makes sense

Pricing is simple and painless. You provide us with an advertisement and you only pay when students actually see your ads.

At Kogo, we charge you by the impact second. An impact second is one second that a student spends watching your advertisement. This way your every penny goes towards gaining a potential customer.

We are unique in that we can guarentee you that your advertisements are only being shown to your target market segment and that you are only paying when they see your ad. You won't find a more efficient and cost-effective service on the market.
